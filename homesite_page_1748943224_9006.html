About this home

This two-story, all concrete block constructed townhome has an open concept downstairs that includes a large open kitchen, a powder room, and a large family room that overlooks a covered lanai. This home comes with all appliances including a refrigerator, built-in dishwasher, electric range, microwave, washer, and dryer.
